Elevator Notice

Please be aware that this is a highly booked condominium on Airbnb, and the building is home to many full-time residents as well. As a result, elevators may take a while to reach your floor — especially during rush hours (mornings and evenings).

We kindly ask that you plan ahead of time whenever you need to head out, so you can avoid any inconvenience or delays. Thank you for your patience and understanding! 🙏

A Note on Shared Living

As with most multi-unit residential buildings, our shared common areas — hallways, stairwells, and corridors — are used by many residents daily. Occasionally, an insect may find its way in, particularly during warmer months. This is completely normal for buildings of this nature.

Please know that we take cleanliness very seriously. Your unit is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before every stay, and we continuously do our best to ensure a fresh and comfortable environment for all our guests.

If you ever notice anything that concerns you during your stay, please don't hesitate to reach out to us — we're always here and happy to help. We truly look forward to hosting you and making your stay as pleasant as possible! 🙏

Room by Room Guide
TV: Use the black remote. Netflix is already logged in and ready to use. If you find it has been logged out, just message us and we'll help you get back in.

AC: Use the white remote. Recommended temperature is 20°C.
Cooking tools: All basic cooking tools are available inside the cabinets — feel free to use them.

Important: Please do not leave dirty plates and utensils in the sink, as this may attract unwanted pests and insects. Do not open cabinets with locks, as we used them for storage of cleaning tools.

Trash: Please dispose of your garbage in the garbage room near the elevators.
Bed: Queen size bed with 4 pillows and duvet

Towels: Fresh towels are provided and placed on top of the bed.
Shower: Hot water comes from the telephone shower head, turn the knob to redirect the water. Adjust the heater thru the separate heater box located on top of the shower.

Supplies: Shampoo, conditioner, body wash, and hair dryer are provided.

Toilet: Please don't flush wipes or paper towels or anything — trash bin is provided for these.
Location: The swimming pool is located on the 8th floor of the building.

Access Fee: Please note that there is a pool access fee:
